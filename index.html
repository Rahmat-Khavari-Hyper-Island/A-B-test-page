Welcome to A/B test page!


This is a simple page designed to demonstrate the concept of A/B testing. The page consists of two sections: a static section and a dynamic section. The static section remains consistent across both versions, A and B. However, the dynamic section varies depending on the chance (fifty-fifty) of loading the page for each user. Additionally, a button has been included for the Call to Action (CTA), and GDPR information is displayed in the console.
